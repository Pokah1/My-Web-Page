Bio

Edikan Odokwo is a frontend software developer passionate about building impactful digital solutions that enhance user experiences and accessibility. With a diploma in Software Engineering from ALT School Africa, he specializes in JavaScript frameworks such as Next-Ss, React, Vue, and TypeScript. He also has a strong foundation in data tools and Microsoft packages, allowing him to work effectively in diverse technical environments.
He has built and contributed to several meaningful projects, including Chatter, a publishing platform similar to Hashnode and Medium, developed with Next.js and TypeScript. He also collaborated with a team of young innovators to build Tranquis, a platform designed to support individuals dealing with depression and mental health challenges by connecting them with medical practitioners for therapy sessions. Currently, he is working on Lera, developing the website for a consulting firm.
Beyond software development, Edikan has gained experience in sales and logistics, having worked at ABC Logistics, where he started as a sales representative and was later promoted to Supervisor for International Courier Operations. This background has sharpened his communication, problem-solving, and leadership skills.

With a strong passion for continuous learning and innovation, Edikan Odokwo is committed to building technology-driven solutions while leveraging his diverse expertise to drive impactful change.
